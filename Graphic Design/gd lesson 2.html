Working with Levels

To apply a level adjustment to a layer, click where the icon is circled in blue.  To edit the blend modes, click the drop down menu circled in red.

Homework Assignment

Create 2 images:
  1. Make an image that consists of 2+ visible layers and merge them.  Add a filter to your image. Use the clipping mask function to frame your layered image around a shape or flat icon.  Use a complementary color background by using the color dropper tool and the Adobe color wheel website. If you can relate it to social justice somehow -- even better! Export your image through the "Save for Web" option as a JPEG.
  1. Select an image and apply levels on it.  Change the blending option.  Use the clip-masking tool to make the fill of the text your filtered image. Use a complementary color background by using the color dropper tool and the Adobe color wheel website. Export your image through the "Save for Web" option as a JPEG.
