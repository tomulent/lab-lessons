### What is it? Import and export your contacts from one email account to another, making it easy to merge contacts lists in a time-efficient way! ### How to use it: 1. Sign in to Gmail. 2. Click **Gmail** at the top-left corner of your Gmail page, then choose **Contacts**. ![image](http://labs.everettprogram.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/skitch_iphoto.export.skitch3.png) 3. From the **More** drop-down menu, select** Export...** ![image](http://labs.everettprogram.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/skitch_iphoto.export.skitch-22.png) 4. Choose whether to export all contacts or only one group. ![image](http://labs.everettprogram.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/skitch_iphoto.export.skitch-32.png) 5. Select the format in which you'd like to export your contacts' information. Please note, some of these formats can lose some contact information.
  • To transfer contacts between Google accounts, use the **Google CSV** This is the recommended way to back up your Google Contacts.
  • To transfer contacts to Outlook, Yahoo! Mail, Hotmail, or various other apps, use the **Outlook CSV **
  • To transfer contacts to Apple Address Book, use the **vCard **format
6. Click **Export  ** 7. Choose **Save to Disk** then click **OK**. 8. Select a location to save your file, and click **OK**. 9.** Importing **contacts into gmail is a bit more complicated, but you can learn it step by step [here](http://www.howtogeek.com/201988/how-to-import-export-contacts-between-outlook-and-gmail/).
