## Homework Assignment Create 2 images:
  1. Make an image that consists of 2+ visible layers and merge them.  Add a filter to your image. Use the clipping mask function to frame your layered image around a shape or [flat icon](http://flaticon.com).  Use a complementary color background by using the color dropper tool and the [Adobe color wheel](https://color.adobe.com/) website. If you can relate it to social justice somehow -- even better! Export your image through the "Save for Web" option as a JPEG.
