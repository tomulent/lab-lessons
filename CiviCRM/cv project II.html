## Other Civi Components The only reason I'm writing this section of the lesson is because I want to let you know how much more you can do with CiviCRM. I hope this inspires you to learn more about CiviCRM, and help organizations with the knowledge you acquired.

[CiviReport ](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/reporting/what-is-civireport/)

CiviReport is the component that allows you to create and view brief summaries of your data. this component integrates with other CiviCRM components. For instance, you can generate a list of  contacts in a group or with a certain tag, create a monthly summary of the contributions you received, etc. And these reports can be emailed directly from CiviCRM or exported.

[CiviCase ](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/case-management/what-is-civicase/)

CiviCase primarily works with the "Activity" tool in CiviCRM. It documents more complex interactions between you and your constituent or staff. The "Activity" function allows you to keep records of interviews, meetings, phone calls and more, and schedule followups. By using CiviCase and Activity, users increase efficiency and have better workflow.

[CiviCampaign  ](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/campaign/what-is-civicampaign/)

When you're organizing a campaign, a lot goes into it. You gather a group of people to work on the campaign, send out emails to your supporters, plan events to raise fund, etc. You already know how to do all of them on CiviCRM, but you kind of need something to connect them together to be more organized. Well, that's when CiviCampaign comes to save your life. According to the[ CiviCRM Users Book](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/campaign/what-is-civicampaign/), "CiviCampaign lets you link together events, mailings, activities, and contributions under one "umbrella" so that you can track the progress of all your efforts towards one programmatic goal or campaign."  CiviCampaign also include two other components: [CiviSurvey](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/survey/what-is-civisurvey/) and [Civipetition](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/petition/what-is-civipetition/).

[CiviGrant](http://book.civicrm.org/user/current/grants/what-is-civigrant/)

If your organization gives out fund or grants, like the Everett Program does, you know CiviContribute is not enough to keep track of your monetary flow.
