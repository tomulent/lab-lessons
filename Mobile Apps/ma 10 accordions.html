[Collapsibles](http://view.jquerymobile.com/1.3.1/dist/demos/widgets/collapsibles/)

Before we do accordions, we need to understand what makes them up, which are ui widgets called "collapsibles". These are widgets that look like buttons that  expand to reveal content they're hiding. Click [here](http://view.jquerymobile.com/1.3.1/dist/demos/widgets/collapsibles/) to see what they are. They're extremely simple to create. You just need 3 things:
  1. A <div> with the attribute **data-role="collapsible"**
  2. As the first thing in the <div> an <h1> that will be the collapsible's lable
  3. Some content to go inside the <div>
That's really it. Take the following code as an example and see what it produces [sourcecode language="html"] <div data-role="collapsible"> <h1>This is a heading</h1> <p>Hello World!</p> </div> [/sourcecode] [caption id="attachment_1711" align="aligncenter" width="300"] Collapsed[/caption] [caption id="attachment_1712" align="aligncenter" width="300"] Expanded[/caption] ### Styling/Theming You can alter colors, borders, and backgrounds of course.
