When you buy your domain with Lithium, they send you a username and password to access the backend of your site. They're both strings of random characters. The username cannot be change, but fortunately the password can.

Login to Lithium Client Area

Either go to www.lithiumhosting.com and click "Sign In" in the upper right corner. Or go straight toÂ https://lithiumhosting.com/billing/clientarea.php. Input the email and password you used to set up your account.

Mouse Over "Services and Domains" and Select "Your Services"

Next to Your Domain, Hit "View Details"

Select "Change Password"

Need we say more? Now you should be able to log in to your cPanel without going to your email and copy/pasting a huge string of letters!
